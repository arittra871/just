What Is " Teacher " ?

A teacher is a person, who provides education for people; one who teaches or instructs. The role of teacher is often formal and ongoing, carried out at a school or other place of formal education. A teacher is a person who helps others to acquire knowledge, competences or values.Teacher is a designation for the office, position, and profession for someone who devotes himself in the field of education through patterned educational interaction, formal and systematic.
